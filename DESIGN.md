# Project Brief: "E-Commerce" Mobile App
## 1\. Project Overview
A high-fidelity Flutter mobile application designed for a premium retail experience. The app features a modern, clean aesthetic with a focus on visual hierarchy and a dual-theme system (Light & Dark) to ensure accessibility and professional appeal across all user environments.
## 2\. Brand Identity
* **Brand Name:** E-Commerce / LuxeShop
* **Design System:** Modern Retail Collective
* **Primary Accent (Light):** Deep Teal (#0F766E)
* **Primary Accent (Dark):** Vibrant Aqua (#14B8A6)
* **Typography:** Be Vietnam Pro / Poppins (Modern, clean sans-serif)
* **Visual Style:** Premium, high-contrast, card-based layout with rounded corners (16px) and subtle elevations.
## 3\. Core Features & User Flows
### A. Discovery & Onboarding
* **Premium Splash Screen:** Animated brand reveal with centered logo and subtle gradients.
* **3-Step Onboarding:**
  1. **Discover Products:** Showcase of the extensive catalog.
  1. **Easy Checkout:** Emphasis on the seamless purchase flow.
  1. **Track Orders:** Real-time visibility into shipping status.
* **Search & Filter:** Intelligent search bar with recent history, popular categories, and a robust filtering bottom sheet (Price, Category, Rating, Sort).
### B. Shopping Experience
* **Home Screen:** Hero banners, featured collections (Summer Collection), and trending product grids.
* **Product Detail:** High-resolution product showcase, color selectors, and floating "Add to Cart" CTA.
* **Category Browsing:** Specialized views for collections (e.g., Men's Wear) with quick-sort chips.
* **Wishlist:** Dedicated area for saved items with quick "Add to Cart" functionality.
### C. Checkout & Post-Purchase
* **Shopping Cart:** Detailed breakdown of items, quantities, and clear subtotal/total calculations.
* **Secure Checkout:** Multi-step process (Shipping, Payment, Review) with trust indicators.
* **Order History:** Comprehensive list of past orders with status badges (Shipped, Delivered, Processing).
* **Order Tracking:** Live visual timeline with step-by-step progress indicators.
### D. User Account & Management
* **User Profile:** Centralized hub for orders, wishlist count, and account settings.
* **Address Management:** Modern card-based management for multiple shipping addresses.
* **Notification Center:** Categorized alerts (Blue: Shipping, Orange: Promos, Green: Delivery) with unread indicators.
## 4\. Technical Specifications
* **Platform:** Flutter (iOS & Android)
* **Screen Dimensions:** 390x844 (Mobile)
* **Themes:**
  * **Light Mode:** White (#FFFFFF) / Light Teal (#F0FDFA) gradients.
  * **Dark Mode:** Deep Navy (#0F172A) / Dark Slate (#1E293B) surfaces.
* **UI Components:** Custom-built Bottom Navigation Bar (4-5 destinations), centered Top App Bar, and consistent card components.
## 5\. Visual Artifacts
The project includes a comprehensive set of screens covering the entire end-to-end user journey, validated through side-by-side theme comparisons and a unified Dark Mode gallery.
# Project Brief: "E-Commerce / LuxeShop" Mobile App
## 1\. Executive Summary
A premium, high-fidelity mobile commerce application designed to provide a seamless shopping experience across iOS and Android platforms. Built with Flutter, the app emphasizes visual clarity, intuitive navigation, and a sophisticated dual-theme (Light & Dark) interface tailored for high-end retail.
## 2\. Brand Identity & Design System
* **Brand Name:** LuxeShop (E-Commerce)
* **Design System:** Modern Retail Collective
* **Aesthetic Direction:** Clean, minimalist, and premium. Focus on high-quality product photography and a card-based layout with rounded corners (16px).
* **Color Palette:**
  * **Light Theme:** Background: #FFFFFF | Accent: Deep Teal (#0F766E) | Secondary: #F9FAFB
  * **Dark Theme:** Background: #0F172A | Accent: Vibrant Aqua (#14B8A6) | Secondary: #1E293B
* **Typography:** Be Vietnam Pro / Poppins (Modern sans-serif)
* **Iconography:** Thin-stroke geometric icons, semantic color coding for alerts (Blue: Shipping, Orange: Promo, Green: Delivery).
## 3\. Core Features & User Flows
### A. Onboarding & Discovery
* **Animated Splash Screen:** High-impact brand reveal with centered logo and fluid loading states.
* **3-Step Walkthrough:** Introduces "Discover Products," "Seamless Checkout," and "Real-time Tracking."
* **Search & Intelligence:** Smart search with history, popular category chips, and a robust multi-criteria filtering system (Price, Rating, Sort).
### B. Shopping Experience
* **Curated Home Screen:** Hero banners for seasonal collections, trending products, and personalized recommendations.
* **Advanced Browsing:** Category-specific views (e.g., Men's Wear) with quick-sort chips and endless scroll.
* **Product Showcase:** High-resolution galleries, color selectors, and persistent "Add to Cart" CTAs.
* **Wishlist:** Dedicated favorites management with quick-add functionality.
### C. Checkout & Operations
* **Streamlined Cart:** Detailed line items with easy quantity adjustments and transparent pricing breakdown.
* **Multi-Step Checkout:** Secure flow covering Shipping, Payment, and Review with integrated trust indicators.
* **Address Management:** Card-based interface for managing multiple shipping and billing addresses.
### D. Post-Purchase Engagement
* **Order Tracking:** Live visual timeline with milestone indicators (Confirmed, Shipped, Out for Delivery, Delivered).
* **Notification Center:** Categorized alerts for order updates, flash sales, and security notifications.
* **User Profile:** Centralized hub for order history, settings, and account management.
## 4\. Technical Specifications
* **Platform:** Cross-platform Flutter (Mobile)
* **Target Resolution:** 390x844 (Mobile-first responsive)
* **Navigation:** Fixed 4-5 destination Bottom Navigation Bar + Small Center-Aligned Top App Bar.
* **Theming:** Dynamic system-level theme switching support.
## 5\. Success Metrics
* **Engagement:** High browse-to-cart conversion rate through optimized discovery.
* **Retention:** Simplified account management and order tracking transparency.
* **Accessibility:** High contrast ratios in both themes and legible typography scales.
